## Overview

NovaTech offers two support tiers to meet the needs of organizations of all sizes. **Standard Support** is included with every NovaTech Platform license. **Premium Support** is available as an add-on for organizations that require faster response times, dedicated support resources, and 24/7 critical issue coverage.

## Pricing

### Standard Support

Standard Support is **included at no additional cost** with every NovaTech Platform license.

### Premium Support

Premium Support is priced as a **per-user per-month add-on** to your NovaTech Platform license:

| User Count | Monthly Price (per user) | Annual Price (per user/month) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 – 500 users | $15.00 / user / month | $12.75 / user / month (15% discount) |
| 501 – 2,000 users | $12.00 / user / month | $10.20 / user / month (15% discount) |
| 2,001+ users | $9.00 / user / month | $7.65 / user / month (15% discount) |

**Pricing Notes:**
- Prices are in USD.
- User count is based on **licensed users** (active + inactive), not concurrent users.
- Annual commitment requires a 12-month minimum contract.
- Volume pricing tiers are applied to the **entire** user count (not incrementally). For example, a customer with 600 users pays $12.00/user/month for all 600 users.
- Pricing is subject to annual review. Existing contracts are honored at the contracted rate for the duration of the term.

## Upgrading Your Support Tier

Customers can upgrade from Standard to Premium Support at any time:

1. Contact your Account Manager or email sales@novatech.com.
2. Premium Support activation takes effect within 2 business days of contract execution.
3. Existing open tickets will be re-evaluated under Premium SLA terms from the activation date.
4. Downgrading from Premium to Standard requires 90 days' written notice before contract renewal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I have Premium Support for only certain users?**  
A: No, Premium Support is applied at the tenant level. All licensed users in a tenant share the same support tier.

**Q: Does Premium Support include product upgrades?**  
A: Product upgrades are included with both Standard and Premium Support as part of your NovaTech Platform license. Premium Support provides 48-hour early access to hotfixes.

**Q: What happens if we exceed the included Custom Integration Support hours?**  
A: Additional hours are billed at $250/hour, invoiced monthly.

**Q: Is there a trial period for Premium Support?**  
A: Yes, new customers can try Premium Support free for 30 days. Contact sales@novatech.com to activate.
